Importance of the Quran

The Holy Quran is the final and complete revelation from Allah to humanity. It provides guidance, wisdom, and clarity for those seeking the truth and a righteous path.

Every verse of the Quran holds deep meaning and brings peace to the heart. It is our duty as Muslims to read, understand, and live by its teachings.

"This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah." (Surah Al-Baqarah: 2)
